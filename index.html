What We Offer

Our Flooring Range

From classic hardwood to modern hybrid options, we supply and install flooring that suits your style and budget.

Hybrid Flooring

The best of both worlds — 100% waterproof with the warmth of timber. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic family areas.

Laminate Flooring

Stylish and scratch-resistant at a budget-friendly price point. Mimics the look of hardwood and suits high-traffic rooms beautifully.

Engineered Flooring

A premium timber look with enhanced stability. Engineered boards are ideal for underfloor heating and areas with seasonal humidity changes.

Parquetry

Distinctive geometric patterns that make a real statement. Our parquetry installations bring an artisan quality and unique personality to any room.

Carpet

Soft underfoot and available in a wide range of textures, colours, and pile heights. Perfect for bedrooms, living areas, and anywhere comfort is a priority.

Commercial Vinyl

Tough, low-maintenance, and built to handle heavy foot traffic. Our commercial vinyl range suits retail, offices, healthcare, and industrial environments.

Installation Services

Expert supply-and-install across the whole Gippsland region. We handle everything from subfloor preparation to the finishing touches.
